A Tribute to A.P.J Abdul Kalam

1931-2015

Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam, known as the Missile Man of India, was a renowned scientist and the 11th President of India. His contributions to aerospace science, particularly in India's missile and nuclear programs, earned him global recognition.

He inspired millions of youth through his speeches, books, and vision for a developed India. His humility, dedication, and belief in education left a legacy that continues to motivate generations.

Famous quotes and line

  • “If you want to shine like a sun, first burn like a sun.”
  • “Don’t take rest after your first victory because if you fail in second, more lips are waiting to say that your first victory was just luck.”
